Dataset Features

⚖️

Passenger Class

Travel class (1st, 2nd, or 3rd) indicating ticket type and amenities

♀️♂️

Passenger Gender

Male or Female - critical demographic variable

🎂

Age

Passenger age in years (some estimates where not recorded)

👫

Siblings & Spouses

Count of relatives in immediate family aboard the ship

👨‍👩‍👧

Parents & Children

Number of parents or children traveling with the passenger

💰

Fare Amount

Cost of ticket in pounds sterling - varies by class and amenities

⛴️

Port of Embarkation

Boarding location (Southampton, Cherbourg, or Queenstown)

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Size

Total family members aboard (SibSp + Parch + 1)

🏠

Is Alone

Binary flag indicating if passenger traveled alone

🛏️

Has Cabin

Whether cabin number was recorded for the passenger

📝

Title

Passenger title extracted from name (Mr, Mrs, Miss, Master)

📊

Class × Fare

Interaction feature: Pclass multiplied by Fare

📈

Age × Fare

Interaction feature: Age multiplied by Fare
